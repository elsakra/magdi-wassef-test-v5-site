Dr. Magdi Wassef has been practicing internal medicine for over thirty years. He graduated from Alexandria Medical School in Egypt in 1985. He then traveled to Wales, United Kingdom, for his internal medicine residency in 1989. From there, his journey brought him to the United States where he completed his residency at Methodist University Hospital. After completing his residency, Dr. Wassef practiced in Tallahatchie General Hospital in Charleston, MS from 1994 to 1996 and then moved back to Memphis where he started Southaven Internal Medicine in 1996. Dr. Wassef is a member of the American College of Physicians, the American Medical Association, and is board certified in internal medicine. He is married to Amira and they have two college aged children, Megan and David. When he is not working, Dr. Wassef enjoys spending time with his family and friends and serving in his church.
